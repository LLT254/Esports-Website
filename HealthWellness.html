About

Diet: Most gamers are sitting in chairs for extensive periods of time, avoiding foods in high calorie count or something that can reduce the risk of blood related illnesses (heart attacks, high blood pressure, atherosclerosis, and strokes) would be an ideal diet for someone in esports.

Week 1;  In all of the weeks Sunday will work as a cheat day where you can eat whatever you want. During the week all foods have been picked with meal prepping in mind, you wont need to make trips to the store every day which makes following this plan very convenient.

Week 2;  You will be much more familiarized with the diet by this point, getting used to knowing the proper servings will make meal prepping easier. Leftovers are expected, you can create your own blends by swapping out sides with whatever you have from the previous days (make sure it is a food that can be stored).
